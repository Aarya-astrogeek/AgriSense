AGRI-PRICE INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
PRODUCT FRAMEWORK DOCUMENT

--------------------------------------------------

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
This product is an end-to-end agri-market intelligence system designed to forecast crop prices and convert them into actionable business insights. The platform supports decision-making for procurement, pricing, and risk management across states, crops, and markets.

--------------------------------------------------

2. PRODUCT OBJECTIVES
- Forecast agricultural commodity prices across regions
- Identify high-margin crop and state opportunities
- Assess regional risk and volatility
- Enable data-driven procurement decisions
- Provide a scalable framework for new crops and regions

--------------------------------------------------

3. TARGET USERS
- Farmers
- Agri-traders and wholesalers
- Procurement teams
- Agri-business companies
- Policy makers and analysts

--------------------------------------------------

4. MODULAR PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE

4.1 Data Ingestion Layer
- Historical crop price data
- Market-level price datasets
- State and commodity identifiers

Output:
- Cleaned and standardized CSV datasets

--------------------------------------------------

4.2 Forecasting & Modeling Layer
- Time-series forecasting for crop prices
- Margin forecasting models
- Accuracy evaluation and tracking
- Bucket-based modeling for different crop behaviors

Output:
- Price forecasts
- Margin projections
- Model accuracy metrics

--------------------------------------------------

4.3 Analytics & Intelligence Layer
- Margin opportunity analysis
- State-wise performance index calculation
- Risk zone classification
- Procurement signal generation (Buy / Hold / Avoid)

Output:
- Business intelligence datasets

--------------------------------------------------

4.4 Visualization & Decision Layer
- Interactive dashboards (Power BI / Tableau)
- State-wise maps and rankings
- Commodity and market filters
- Trend and comparison charts

Output:
- Business-ready interactive dashboard

--------------------------------------------------

5. SCALABILITY & EXTENSIBILITY
- New crops can be added by extending input data
- New states or markets are automatically reflected
- Forecasting models can be upgraded independently
- Dashboard structure remains unchanged

--------------------------------------------------

6. BUSINESS USE CASES
- Procurement planning and sourcing decisions
- Identification of high-profit crops and regions
- Risk-aware pricing strategies
- Market expansion and entry analysis

--------------------------------------------------

7. DELIVERABLES
- Interactive dashboard (Power BI)
- Forecast and analytics CSV files
- Product framework document (this file)
- Modular and scalable system design

--------------------------------------------------

8. FUTURE ENHANCEMENTS
- Real-time data integration
- Weather and climate signal incorporation
- Automated model retraining
- Mobile-friendly dashboards
- Role-based views for different stakeholders

--------------------------------------------------
